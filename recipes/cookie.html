This is a beautiful campfire cookie recipe to make on a cool late night by the campfire! A recipe sure to make whoever you eat with think you;re a cooking aficionado

These cookie sandwiches will be made with preexisting cookies for the simplicity of camplife but feel free to follow another one of our recipes to make your own

Without further ado, lets get started!

Ingredients

  • 5 (1 ounce) squares chopped white chocolate
  • 20 oatmeal cookies
  • 1 (1.55 ounce) bar milk chocolate candy
  • 1 cup miniature marshmallows

Directions

  1. Melt white chocolate in a double boiler, spread evenly over the bottoms of the cookies. Break up the milk chocolate bar into 10 pieces, and place 1 piece onto the white chocolate side of 10 cookies. Place the marshmallows on top of the milk chocolate. Top with the remaining cookies, creating a chocolate-marshmallow sandwich.
  2. Toast cookies over a campfire, or stove using tongs until the chocolate and marshmallow has melted, about 3 minutes.

Congratulations, you're done! You can now enjoy impressing all your friends on the camp site!
