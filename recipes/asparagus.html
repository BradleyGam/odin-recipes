Grilled Asparagus

Go Back

This is a great grilled asparagus recipe to make as a side course! A recipe sure to make whoever you eat with think you're a cooking aficionado

Now for most this dish won't stand alone so feel free to follow one of our other recipes such as grilled chicken or pasta

Without further ado, lets get started!

Ingredients

  • 1 pound fresh asparagus spears, trimmed
  • 1 tablespoon olive oil
  • salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Preheat an outdoor grill for high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  2. Lightly coat the asparagus spears with olive oil. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Cook asparagus on the preheated grill, turning often until lightly charred and fork-tender about 3 to 5 minutes.

Congratulations, you're done! You can now enjoy impressing all your next dinner guests!
